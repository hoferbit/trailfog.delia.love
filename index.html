Features

Three tabs. A lifetime of footprints.

No setup needed — just open and walk.

📍

Record

Opens straight to your location. One tap to start — the fog clears in real time as you walk, with mileage views for day / week / month / year / lifetime.

▶

Replay

Replay any past session — the route grows back at a steady pace alongside a real 3D rolling globe, like walking it all over again.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Stays local by default. Flip the iCloud toggle any time to sync across devices — your data lives only in your own private iCloud space.

The fog is the map of your life.

The whole world starts covered in fog. Walk somewhere, and the fog clears there — every street, every city you've been to stays lit on your own map, permanently.

No check-ins, no posts to prove where you've been. This map exists for no one but you.

The more you walk, the brighter your world gets.
